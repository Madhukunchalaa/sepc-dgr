Fault /Breakdown / Defect / Event
On 06-05-2025 at 23:25 hr, Slag conveyor hydraulic drive pressure raised instantly from 38 bar to max 180 bar & got immobilized. At driveshaft, scrapper chain got disengaged from DE sprocket wheel (Mill-30 side) & no movement. DE side chain derailed from guide channel and five scrapper plates got inclined.

Any HSE impact?
Nil

Conditions prior to failure
*Unit load was 170 MW, and boiler was in service with four mills. 
*Slag conveyor hydraulic pressure reached 70 bar around 20:30 hrs due to high bottom ash dislodged during load backdown & got normalized to 35 bar about half-an-hour.
*During the time of high bottom ash dislodge, slag conveyor drain valves were operated to drain excess ash and flushed. 

Sequence of Events of failure

23:25 hrs on 06-05-2025
Slag conveyor hydraulic drive pressure instantly spiked to 180 bar with H1 & H2 alarms, and zero speed switch acted. No movement in local & found chain disengaged at drive shaft DE sprocket wheel.

02:15 hrs on 07-05-2025
Unit load reduction started from 170 MW for Slag conveyor restoration & desynchronized at 02:43 hrs

Observations / Findings

*After boiler shut-down, PTW was taken and Slag conveyor repair works started.
*Ash accumulation was cleared by opening two man-doors at left and right side, and rear door.
*After clearing the ash, found three no’s of scrapper chain’s guide channel pieces (3m & 1m length angle bars) entrapped in return side guide wheel at slanting portion near Mill-30.
*The entrapped angle bars between guide-wheel and chain were removed, scrapper chain was repositioned, and chain tension checked.

why why analysis

Why1
Why Slag conveyor got immobilized?


Due to scrapper chain disengaged from DE sprocket wheel of drive shaft and derailing of scrapper chain from guides.

Why2
Why did the scrapper chain get disengaged from sprocket?


The guide channel got detached, travelled and entrapped between the guide wheel and chain and obstructed the movement of scrapper chain. This caused an imbalance in load and one of the two chains at DE got disengaged from sprocket.

Why3
Why did the guide channel get detached?


The carrying side guide channel possibly worn out and detached due to weldment weakness caused by abrasion, thermal fatigue and prolonged exposure to high temperature.

Direct cause
Guide channel damage, removal and struck-up in guide-wheel caused the slag conveyor immobilization.

Underlying cause
Inaccessibility of slag conveyor’s carrying side below the furnace and return side for regular inspection and preventive maintenance, which can be done only after boiler shutdown, cooling and pulling out the slag conveyor.

Root cause
The guide channel weld joint failure possibly caused by wear and tears, abrasion due to high bottom ash, clinker falling, and thermal fatigue.

Preventive actions taken to avoid fault / breakdown / defect / event
As the bottom ash generation from the lignite received from Mines-II is comparatively high, to prevent the ash accumulation inside the slag conveyor, carrying side and return side drain valves were frequently operated and flushed whenever the hydraulic drive pressure goes high. 

Corrective actions taken for restoration
*After removing the angle bars, the scrapper chain was repositioned, and the trial run was normal. 
*Unit was restarted and synchronized at 23:08 hrs on the same day (07-05-2025)

Recommendations to avoid recurrence
*Ensure full welding (and no tag welding) of support angle bars for its rigidity and quality of welding by carrying out appropriate NDT checks, so as to withstand during high abrasive bottom ash collection.
*Inspection of slag conveyor for any signs of worn-out whenever the opportunity arises.
*Water lancing to dislodge slag and to avoid building up.
*Ensuring effective fuel additive dosing.

Action plan target date
Continuous
Action owners
O&M

Similar incidents occurred in this calendar year
On 20-02-2025, one of the carrying side wear plates got detached and struck up at return side. It was removed without requiring boiler shutdown as it is trapped at the exposed portion of slag conveyor.

Comments / Learnings / Feedback if any
Planning of the maintenance accordingly to mitigate the effects of high bottom ash collection.
